About DreamMaker

DreamMaker is a web application designed to help users manage their dreams, goals, todos, and events in an organized manner. With DreamMaker, users can set and track their dreams, create todos associated with their dreams, and manage events relevant to their goals.

Why Choose DreamMaker?

Goal Management

Easily create, track, and manage your goals with DreamMaker.

Todo Management

Stay organized by managing todos associated with your goals.

Event Management

Keep track of important events related to your goals and dreams.

Simple UI/UX

DreamMaker's simple and intuitive design provides good user interface.

How It Works

Step 1: Sign Up

Create your account on DreamMaker by signing up with your email and password.

Step 2: Set Goals

Define your dreams and goals by setting them up in DreamMaker's intuitive interface.

Step 3: Manage Tasks

Stay organized by managing tasks associated with your goals and tracking your progress.
